Drinks

  • green delight

    The green delight is a green jelly, made from the leaves of a plant, put in fresh coconut milk mixed with some palm sugar and topped with ice cubes. Refreshingly healthy!

  • orange juice

    Orange ice is a refreshing drink. This drink menu will not only eliminate thirst but will also fulfill the need for vitamin C in the body.

  • mix coconut iced

    mix coconut iced is a drink served cold with the contents of coconut meat and a mixture of syrup and sweetened condensed milk.

  • ice kuwut

    Ice kuwut is a typical Balinese drink with the basic ingredients of young coconut water mixed with melon, lime, and basil.

  • iced tea

    Iced Tea is a beverage that is cooled with ice cubes. Iced tea is added with flavors like jasmine, and fruits such as lemonade, cherries, and strawberries.
