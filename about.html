About Jineng Bali

Inspired by the rich culinary taste in Bali, Jineng Bali is here to answer it all. This restaurant in Seminyak Bali has the theme of typical Balinese dishes ranging from chicken betutu, pork roll, satay wrapped and others. Jineng Bali was founded in 2020.

Jineng Bali serves a variety of dishes that are so delicious. Jineng Bali is proud to present dishes born from perfectly authentic recipes. The menus are always liked by the tongue of visitors from any country.

For places to eat, visitors eat at a place called "Jineng". for interior decoration offered by Jineng Bali is elegant and comfortable. You can find the dominance of dark wood, large sofas, high curtains, and classic chandeliers in the restaurant room. Jineng Bali is always full of visitors, especially on weekends because Jineng Bali is the right place to gather with family or couples.

Please reserve a place before dining here.
