Foods

  • ayam betutu

    Ayam betutu or betutu chicken is chicken meat filled with herbs and then roasted in a husk fire. Spices in making betutu chicken consist of onion, candlenut, garlic, turmeric, galangal, ginger, cayenne pepper, lemongrass, brown sugar, orange leaves, bay leaves, and coconut oil.

  • babi guling

    Babi guling or pork bolsters are one of the special foods from the Island of the Gods. Balinese pork roll culinary is a food menu serving sliced ​​pork bolsters with some other Balinese specialties for accompanying rice.

  • bebek betutu

    This dish was used during traditional ceremonies or religious ceremonies. The duck meat that used to make betutu duck is a local duck.

  • nasi campur

    Called mixed rice because it consists of white rice mixed with various side dishes, such as fried sauce, shredded, fried tofu, fried fish, eggs and others

  • sate lilit

    Sate lilit is a typical Balinese food that is processed using meat ingredients. The meat will be mashed and mixed with grated coconut, coconut milk, lime, onion, and pepper. The mixed meat will be wrapped in bamboo, lemongrass or sugar cane and finally baked
